Dataset

The model is trained using environmental sensor data that influences indoor air quality.

🌡 Temperature

Affects air density and indoor ventilation conditions.

💧 Humidity

High humidity can indicate poor ventilation and air stagnation.

💡 Light

Used as an indirect indicator of human activity inside the room.

👥 Occupancy

More people → higher CO₂ due to breathing.

🌿 CO₂ Levels

Target variable used to train and evaluate the model.

These features help the model learn relationships between environment and air quality.
